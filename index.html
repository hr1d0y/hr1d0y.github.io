Privacy Policy

Dev Fetch  ·  Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dev Fetch ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Dev Fetch mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google or create an account with email and password, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Folder names: Names you give to saved article folders.

Information collected automatically

  • Read history: Articles and papers you open are recorded to power your reading history and personalise your feed.
  • Saved articles: Articles you save to folders are stored on our servers linked to your account.
  • Topic preferences: Topics you select during onboarding or in settings.
  • Notification preferences: Keywords and topics you configure for push alerts.
  • FCM token: A Firebase Cloud Messaging token is stored to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Subscription status: Whether you hold an active Dev Fetch Premium subscription, verified via Google Play.

Information from third parties

  • Google Sign-In: We receive basic profile data (name, email, Google ID) from Google OAuth when you authenticate.
  • Google Play Billing: When you purchase a subscription, Google Play provides us with a purchase token to verify and activate your subscription. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • Google AdMob: If you are not a premium subscriber, ads are served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • Personalise your news feed based on your selected topics
  • Sync your saved articles, folders, and read history across sessions
  • Deliver push notifications for topics and keywords you subscribe to
  • Verify and manage your Premium subscription
  • Show ads to non-premium users via Google AdMob
  • Improve the App's content and features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on our servers hosted in the European Union. We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our servers within 30 days.

4. Subscriptions

Dev Fetch Premium is offered as a monthly or yearly auto-renewing subscription through Google Play Billing.

  • Subscription payments are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • To cancel, manage your subscription through the Google Play Store app on your device.
  • Refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policy.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we store your Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to send you alerts. You can disable notifications at any time from the App's notification settings or your device's system settings.

6. Advertising

Users who have not subscribed to Dev Fetch Premium may see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads through your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google — for authentication, push notifications, ads, and subscription verification.
  • Service providers — infrastructure providers used to host and operate the App, bound by confidentiality obligations.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Dev Fetch is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
